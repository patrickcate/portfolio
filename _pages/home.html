About Me (What I do)

  • Print Design:

    Designing marketing collateral, advertisements, or other visual media, laying out a multi-page book or reports, or creating your brands corporate identity, I do all of it and more. Everything you put your brand on will be tailored to match your company's image and customer market for maximum effectiveness.

  • Web Design:

    I use the latest tools to design creative and user friendly websites that work on any screen size or device. While other designers and developers see this as something to be tacked on for an additional expense, I don't. There isn't a separate mobile Internet, so why should you have a separate mobile website? With me, you won't.

  • Drupal Theming:

    When your website needs a robust CMS like Drupal, I'm your man to create or refine a custom theme that reflects and enhances your brand/product/service. No cookie-cutter themes here. With a strong background in Drupal theming I know the in's and out's of the architecture needed to create an exceptional site design that doesn't compromise the speed and security that Drupal has built in.

  • Brand Identity:

    A picture is worth a thousand words, and your company's logo can be worth much more. I work closely to craft a logo and overall brand identity that reflects the purpose and image you want to project. This often requires close collaboration and research of target markets and competitors, you'll get that from me and more.

  • App UI Design:

    Sometimes a product or service requires the tight integration and speed of a smartphone or tablet hardware. In those cases an easy to use interface that provides a positive and memorable user experience can be the difference between an app store failure and success. I design the latter.

  • E-Mail Design:

    E-Mail isn't dead, it just needs a recharge. Whether viewing on your phone, tablet, or desktop, I design e-mail templates that look good in all of them and match your brand and target audience perfectly. Make your e-mail to good looking to end up in a junk folder.
